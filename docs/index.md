## Privacy Policy for Liqui2Cal Chrome Extension

### Introduction

Thank you for using the Liqui2Cal Chrome Extension. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our extension.

### Information We Collect

**We do not collect any user data.** Liqui2Cal operates entirely within your browser and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

### How the Extension Works

- Liqui2Cal reads publicly available match schedule data (timestamps, player names, tournament info) from Liquipedia pages you visit.
- When you click "Add to Calendar", the extension generates a Google Calendar URL and opens it in a new tab. No data is sent to any server.

### Data Storage

- **No local storage:** The extension does not use browser storage, cookies, or any local data persistence.
- **No remote storage:** No data is sent to any external server or third-party service.

### Permissions

The extension requires access to `liquipedia.net` solely to read match schedule information from the page and inject calendar buttons. No other permissions are requested.

### Sharing of Information

We do not share, sell, or distribute any information to third parties because we do not collect any information.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated revision date.
